Raptors announce preseason schedule

August 8, 2002) -- The Toronto Raptors announced Thursday the team will play four games at Air Canada Centre as part of its eight-game 2002 preseason schedule. 

The Raptors will open training camp October 1 in Waterloo, Ontario at RIM Park and play their first preseason game October 8 against the Detroit Pistons at Air Canada Centre. 

In addition to the Pistons, the Raptors will play host to Philadelphia, Boston and the defending Eastern Conference champion New Jersey Nets at Air Canada Centre. The club will also serve as the home team for a neutral site contest against Golden State in El Paso, Texas. 

The Raptors begin the 2002-03 regular season Wednesday, October 30 when they play host to Michael Jordan and the Washington Wizards at Air Canada Centre. 

The complete Toronto Raptors preseason schedule is as follows: 

Tues., Oct. 8 -- Detroit Pistons at Air Canada Centre 7:00 p.m. 
Fri., Oct. 11 -- Chicago United Center Chicago 8:30 p.m.
Sun., Oct. 13 -- Philadelphia Air Canada Centre Toronto 6:00 p.m.
Tues., Oct. 15 -- Cleveland Schottenstein Center Columbus, OH 7:30 p.m.
Fri., Oct. 18 -- New Jersey Air Canada Centre Toronto 7:00 p.m.
Sun., Oct. 20 -- Boston Air Canada Centre Toronto 7:00 p.m.
Tues., Oct. 22 -- Golden State Don Haskins Events Center El Paso, TX 9:30 p.m.
Thurs., Oct. 24 -- Utah Delta Center Salt Lake City 9:00 p.m.
