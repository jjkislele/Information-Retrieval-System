Raptors Activate Bradley

(March 17,2002) --- The Toronto Raptors announced Sunday they placed forward Michael Bradley on the active roster and centre Michael Stewart on the injured list with a hernia. 

Stewart underwent surgery Saturday evening at St. Michael’s Hospital in Toronto to repair the hernia. He is currently resting at his home in Toronto. He had averaged 2.0 points, 2.3 rebounds and 8.5 minutes in 18 games. He is expected to be sidelined for three weeks. 

Bradley had been on the injured list since February 24 with a sprained right ankle. He has appeared in 19 games this season, totaling 13 points and 14 rebounds in 80 minutes.
